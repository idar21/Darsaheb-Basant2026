Ustad Phadder (Al-mashoor Gujranwalia)
Invites you to Lahore Basant 2026!

O ji, tayyari kar lao — Basant aa gayi te naal le aayi Lahore di mashhoor shor sharaba!
Chhattaan te kursiyaan rakho, te neighbour nu dass do ke ajj bohat hullar houna ay!
Patangaan waang ghar di bijli vi kabhi kabhi upar neeche honi ay!
Dor tight rakhiyo jee, nahi taan bachay kehnde phir "Bo kataa!" te hass hass ke dub marrde ne!
Chacha ji kehnde ne: "Je patang na uddi taan hawa nu dosh do — banda kado galti manda ae?"
Te mithai te lemo soda taan Basant da Sunnah ne, oh taan lazmi ay jee!
Lahore di hawa, rang, maza — full kashmakash te full masti!
